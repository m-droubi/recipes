Mshat

Description

mshat is a palestinian fried food ,it's cheap and simple.

ingredients

  • 1 cauliflower, boiled
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 bunch parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • Salt, a pinch
  • Cumin, a pinch
  • Turmeric, a pinch
  • Mixed spices, a pinch
  • Vegetable oil, for frying as needed

Preparation Method

  1. Mash the boiled cauliflower until medium-coarse.
  2. Place the mashed cauliflower in a bowl with the chopped parsley,
    chopped onion, garlic, cumin, salt, spices, flour, and eggs.
  3. Mix the ingredients well until combined.
  4. Shape the mixture into medium-thick patties.
  5. In a large pot of hot oil, fry the patties until cooked through and golden brown.
